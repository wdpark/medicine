**Most Common**
- Intra-axial mass = most likely metastasis
- Primary intra-axial mass
	- Adults: Glioblastoma multiforme
	- Children: Medulloblastoma (PNET)
- Extra-axial mass
	- Meningioma

**Brain Metastasis**
- Most common intra-axial mass
- Primary Malignancies
	- Lung, Breast, Melanoma
- Signs
	- Well-defined
	- Near gray-white junction
	- Multiple or solitary
	- Surrounding edema
	- Enhances with contrast
