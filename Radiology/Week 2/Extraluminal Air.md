**Intraperitoneal (pneumoperitoneum)**
- Causes --> think rupture of air-containing structure
	- Trauma
	- Perforated peptic ulcer
	- Perforated diverticulitis/appendicitis
	- Perforation of carcinoma
	- Post-op 5-7 days
- Signs
	- Crescentic lucency beneath the diaphragm
	- Rigler's Sign
		- Air on both sides of the bowel wall
	- Football Sign
		- Visualization of falciform ligament on liver

**Retroperitoneal**
- Causes
	- Bowel rupture (2/2 ruptured appendicitis or UC)
	- Trauma (blunt or penetrating)
	- Iatrogenic manipulation (surgery or colonoscopy)
	- Foreign body perforation
	- Gas-producing infection (perforated diverticulitis)
