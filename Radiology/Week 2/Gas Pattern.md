**Normal Gas Pattern**
- Source
	- Swallowed air (majority)
	- Bacterial fermentation from food

| Organ       | Gas Normally?     | Air-Fluid Levels |
| ----------- | ----------------- | ---------------- |
| Stomach     | Yes               | Yes              |
| Small bowel | Yes, 1-2 loops    | Yes              |
| Large bowel | Yes, rectosigmoid | No               |

| Small Bowel                        | Large Bowel                                  |
| ---------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Centrally located                  | Peripherally located                         |
| Valvulae extend across lumen       | Haustral markings do NOT extend across lumen |
| Valvulae are spaced close together | Haustral markings are widely spaced apart    |
| Normal diameter 3 cm               | Normal diameter 6 cm (9 cm cecum)            |

**Abnormal Gas Pattern**
- Localized Ileus
	- Focal irritation of loops of bowel --> adjacent inflammation
	- Causes:
		- Cholecystitis
		- Pancreatitis
		- Appendicitis
		- Diverticulitis
