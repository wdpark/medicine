- Definition: Epiphyseal growth plate fractures in children classification system
	- Most vulnerable in growing bone due to shearing injury
	- 30% of all childhood fractures
	- Describes type of fracture, implications for treatment, and likelihood of complications

**Type I**
- Isolated epiphyseal plate injury
- Good prognosis, heals well
- Example: Slipped capital femoral epiphysis (SCFE)
