Assessment:
- Check malignancy/cancer screenings and thiazide/calcitriol/lithium use
- Stones (kidney stones/renal failure)
- Bones (pain, osteoporosis)
- Groans (abdominal pain, constipation)
- Check volume status and mentation
- DDx:
	- Primary hyperPTH
	- CKD
	- Malignancy (SCC lung and head/neck, breast, RCC, myeloma)
	- Sarcoidosis

Plan:
- Low PTH or high concern for malignancy? -> PTHrP, SPEP, UPEP, SFLC, consider pan scan

Treatment:
- Fluids
- Calcitonin 4-8 units/kg for 48 hours if Ca >14 or severe symptoms
- Bisphosphonates if refractory
	- Zolendronic acid 4 mg IV is better than pamidronate 90 mg IV q3-4 weeks
	- Needs 2-4 days for effect
	- Replete vitamin D <20
	- Denosumab if CKD
