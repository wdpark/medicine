**Biochemical markers of Kidney Function**
- Serum creatinine is proportional to muscle mass (young, men, Black)
	- Low muscle mass patient -> low serum creatinine -> perceived as better GFR -> overestimation of GFR

**Estimation of GFR**
- Creatine clearance (CrCl) is a better measure of GFR than serum creatinine, but is affected by accuracy of collection and tubular creatinine secretion
