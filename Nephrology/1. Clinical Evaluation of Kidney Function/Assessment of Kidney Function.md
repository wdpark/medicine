**Biochemical markers of Kidney Function**
- Serum creatinine and serum cystatin C are primary biomarkers of kidney function
- Serum creatinine is proportional to muscle mass (young, men, Black)
	- Therefore, the creatinine of patients with lower muscle mass may overestimate true GFR
- BUN can detect renal hypoperfusion if increase is disproportionate to creatinine
